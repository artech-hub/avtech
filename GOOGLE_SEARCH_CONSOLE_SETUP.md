### Step 1: Google Search Console Setup

2. **Add Property**:

   - Enter: `https://avtechcompany.in`

   - **DNS record method**: Add TXT record to your domain DNS

1. In Google Search Console, go to "Sitemaps"
2. Submit your sitemap URL: `https://avtechcompany.in/sitemap.xml`
3. Click "Submit"
