Our Story

Founded in 2020 by Arvind Verma, AVtech Company emerged from a vision to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and practical business solutions. Our journey started with a passionate vision to deliver innovative solutions across education, construction, healthcare, and enterprise sectors.
